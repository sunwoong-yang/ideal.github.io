IDEA's Education: Vibe Coding Seminar Participation

A public lecture organized by the Ministry of Science and ICT was held for residents of Gyeonggi-do, introducing the history of AI development and demonstrating how to create and publish a website using AI agent technology.

Organizer
Ministry of Science and ICT (MSIT)
Venue
Room 204, Industry-Academia-Research Cluster Building
Date & Time
Jun. 24, 2026, 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM
Topics
History of AI development; website creation & deployment via AI agent (vibe coding)

Participant reviews:

Seunghan Jung — Explored AI agent concepts and the development history behind their emergence, and experienced vibe coding firsthand by building and publishing a website.

Minhyuk Choi — Learned to implement web pages with Vibe Coding and deploy via GitHub, gaining practical skills for AI-assisted project management and personal portfolio building.

Hyungjin Ha — Gained a deeper understanding of AI-powered development, learning that each AI tool has distinct strengths and that effective prompting can quickly turn ideas into practical solutions.
