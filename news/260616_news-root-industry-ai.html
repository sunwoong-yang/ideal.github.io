IDEA LAB Awarded Research Grant for AI-Driven Root Industry Manufacturing

IDEA LAB has been selected as a participating research team in the national R&D project "Building Digital Transformation Technology Foundation for an AI Ecosystem in Root Industries," funded by the Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy (MOTIE), Republic of Korea.

Lead Institution
Korea Institute of Industrial Technology (KITECH)
Funder
Ministry of Trade, Industry and Energy (MOTIE)
Period
Jul. 2026 – Dec. 2030 (4.5 years)
IDEA LAB Funding
500,000,000 KRW
Role
Co-Investigator

The project targets five core root manufacturing processes — die/mold, metal forming, casting, precision machining, and injection/press — and aims to build a comprehensive AI ecosystem by standardizing design, process, and quality data across these industries.

IDEA LAB's contribution focuses on AI-based design automation for these five manufacturing processes. Our team will develop intelligent models that reduce trial-and-error in process design and accelerate convergence to high-quality solutions by combining data-driven learning with domain engineering knowledge.

This project represents a meaningful expansion of IDEA LAB's AI-for-manufacturing research portfolio, extending our physics-guided AI framework to the broader manufacturing sector.
