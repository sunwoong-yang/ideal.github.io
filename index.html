Team Vision

IDEAL Roadmap

Human-Centered Intelligent Design

We are building a studio where AI copilots amplify engineering creativity rather than replace it. Our team vision is to connect researchers, students, and industry partners through an open design culture that values transparency and reproducibility.

  • Translate real-world engineering challenges into collaborative design missions.
  • Prototype responsible AI workflows that keep human intent in the loop.
  • Share reusable playbooks so young researchers can rapidly onboard.
Community Impact

Trustworthy Engineering Intelligence

IDEAL is committed to delivering reliable decision support tools for domains where safety and performance matter. We emphasize measurable gains—faster simulations, better prototypes, and explainable recommendations.

  • Partner with academic labs and startups on high-impact demonstrators.
  • Publish open datasets and tutorials to encourage reproducible research.
  • Mentor the next generation of designers who speak both engineering and AI.

Research Area

Physics-Guided AI Pipelines

Learning with Domain Constraints

We develop hybrid surrogates that blend CFD/FEM knowledge with neural operators to deliver trustworthy predictions even when data is scarce.

  • Multi-fidelity modeling for aerodynamics, rotorcraft, and fluid-structure systems.
  • Uncertainty quantification modules that feed directly into design trade-offs.
Generative Engineering Platforms

Co-Designing with Large Models

Our lab prototypes workflow agents that pair LLM reasoning with CAD, simulation, and optimization loops so that engineers can explore concepts faster.

  • Prompt-to-geometry pipelines that maintain manufacturability constraints.
  • Dashboard analytics that capture design rationale for future reuse.
