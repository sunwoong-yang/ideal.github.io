Appointed Assistant Professor at Hanyang University ERICA

Effective March 1, 2026, I have joined the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Hanyang University ERICA as an Assistant Professor. This appointment officially launches IDEA LAB on campus and opens up new opportunities to build an AI-driven design culture with students and industry partners.

My recent postdoctoral work at KAIST focused on trustworthy AI for aerodynamic applications and is now being transitioned into the IDEA LAB research roadmap. By integrating data-efficient learning, physics-guided modeling, and design automation tools, I plan to accelerate how engineering teams explore concepts, validate ideas, and deploy solutions.

IDEA LAB will continue expanding collaborations across Korea’s aerospace and mobility sectors. We welcome prospective students and industry partners who want to co-create the next generation of intelligent design workflows.
